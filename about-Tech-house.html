The Tech House Story

My name is Tinafi Akawu. I am a fifteen-year-old singer, songwriter, spoken word artist, and tech enthusiast. My passion for technology has been present since I can remember. As a child, I was constantly curious about how smartphones worked—often asking my mom how phone calls were made and how someone at a great distance could hear and answer me instantly.

In primary school, Computer Studies became my favorite subject. This was where I was introduced to basic coding and the fundamentals of how software and hardware interact. My journey took a massive leap forward when I was introduced to text-to-speech screen readers. These tools opened up endless possibilities, enabling me to research any topic, send messages, and navigate mobile devices with ease.

By combining text-to-speech with speech-to-text, I transformed my gadget experience, allowing me to know exactly what was on my screen and type anything with just my voice. In 2023, I saw the need for this technological knowledge to be shared with everyone, leading me to create Tech House.

Starting as a WhatsApp group, Tech House expanded to YouTube and Facebook where I create videos to help others learn. Today, I am focused on making technology truly accessible. My main project, T Vision, uses AI to help visually impaired people navigate their surroundings, while my Music Producer app aims to automate the music production process, blending my life as an artist with my work as an innovator.

Ultimately, I am driven by a desire to learn, create, and make technology more accessible for everyone.
