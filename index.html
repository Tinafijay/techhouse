About Tech House

At Tech House, we're driven by a simple yet powerful mission: to make technology accessible, intuitive, and genuinely helpful for everyone.

Meet Our CEO: Tinafi Akawu

Behind Tech House is Tinafi Akawu, a fifteen-year-old artist and tech enthusiast whose fascination with technology began at a very young age...

Recognizing the universal need for technological understanding, Tinafi founded Tech House in 2023. What began as a dedicated WhatsApp group quickly expanded its reach to prominent social media platforms like YouTube and Facebook.

Tinafi's driving force is a desire to reshape the world through better and more accessible technology. This vision is actively taking shape through ambitious app development projects like T Vision and Music Producer.
