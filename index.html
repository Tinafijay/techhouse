About the Founder

CEO of Tech House — Tinafi Akawu

My name is Tinafi Akawu. I am a fifteen-year-old tech enthusiast, artist, and innovator. Tech House was born from my passion for making technology accessible to everyone through tools like screen readers and AI.

Starting as a WhatsApp group, Tech House expanded to YouTube and Facebook where I create videos to help others learn. Today, I am focused on making technology truly accessible.

My main project, T Vision, uses AI to help visually impaired people navigate their surroundings, while my Music Producer app aims to automate the music production process, blending my life as an artist with my work as an innovator.

Ultimately, I am driven by a desire to learn, create, and make technology more accessible for everyone.
