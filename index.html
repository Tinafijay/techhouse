Our Story

Tinafi Akawu (TinafiJay)

Tech House grew from a deep curiosity. In JSS2, I started "Young Programmers" with friends. In 2023, I renamed it Tech House to build tools for accessibility.

I was always fascinated by how I could be in Abuja and reach someone in Lagos instantly. I want to make sure technology serves everyone, including the visually impaired.
