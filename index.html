Meet Our CEO: Tinafi Akawu

Behind Tech House is Tinafi Akawu, a fifteen-year-old artist and tech enthusiast whose fascination with technology began at a very young age. Tinafi's curiosity sparked from everyday interactions, often wondering how seemingly complex devices, like smartphones, functioned across distances. This early intrigue led to hands-on exploration, navigating mobile devices to play videos and use the camera, and even finding creative ways to overcome challenges by relying on others to read options for more complex tasks.

This passion deepened during primary school, where computer studies became a favorite subject, introducing foundational coding knowledge and understanding the intricate dance between software and hardware. A pivotal moment arrived with the discovery of text-to-speech screen readers, which revolutionized mobile navigation and opened up a world of possibilities for independent research, communication, and exploration. Complementing this, speech-to-text further enhanced the gadget experience, enabling seamless voice-to-text input.

Recognizing the universal need for technological understanding, Tinafi founded Tech House in 2023. What began as a dedicated WhatsApp group quickly expanded its reach to prominent social media platforms like YouTube and Facebook, serving as a hub for regular video content on various tech topics.

Tinafi's driving force is a desire to reshape the world through better and more accessible technology.
