Our Story

Tinafi Akawu (TinafiJay)

Tech House didn't just appear; it grew from a deep curiosity. Even as a child in primary school, I was fascinated by technology. I remember always asking my mom questions about how phones worked. I couldn't understand how I could be in Abuja and reach someone in Lagos instantly, or even call someone all the way in America. I spent my time playing with phones and gadgets, trying to unlock their secrets.

By the time I was in JSS2, I started a WhatsApp group with my friends called "Young Programmers." We loved coding and sharing what we learned. As time went on, my friends moved on to other interests, but my passion only grew. In 2023, I officially renamed the group to Tech House.

It transformed from a small group of friends into a platform where I share the latest tech stories, coding tips, and build apps that focus on accessibility. I want to make sure that technology is useful for everyone, including those with disabilities or visual impairments.
