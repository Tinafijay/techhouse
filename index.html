About the Founder

CEO of Tech House — Tinafi Akawu

I am a fifteen-year-old singer, songwriter, spoken word artist, and tech enthusiast. My passion for tech began with a simple curiosity: How do phone calls work? I would ask my mom how someone far away could hear and answer me instantly.

In primary school, Computer Studies became my favorite subject. It was there I learned the fundamentals of software and hardware. The game-changer for me was the introduction of text-to-speech screen readers and speech-to-text. These accessibility tools didn't just help me navigate devices; they opened up a world of research, communication, and creation.

In 2023, I founded Tech House. It started as a WhatsApp group and has grown into a platform on YouTube and Facebook where I share tech knowledge with everyone. I believe technology should have no barriers.

My current mission is building T Vision, an AI app designed to help visually impaired people navigate the world with ease, and Music Producer, an AI tool that automates the production process—combining my love for art and innovation.
