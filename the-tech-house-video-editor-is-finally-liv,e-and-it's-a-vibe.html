It’s built with **FFmpeg.wasm**, which basically means you’re running a professional-grade video engine right inside your browser. No "uploading to a server and waiting 10 years" nonsense—it's all happening right on your machine.

### Why this thing actually slaps:

* **Auto-Cut Silence:** I added a feature that scans your audio for those awkward "uhhh" moments and long pauses, then just... snips them. It’s like a digital haircut for your videos.

* **The Layer Stack:** You can toss in logos, illustrations, sound effects, background music and B-roll overlays like you're playing Tetris

* **Music Focus Controller:** This is my favorite part. You can focus a BGM track and nudge it around with the keyboard until the beat drops exactly when you want it to.

* **Privacy-First:** Since it’s all local, your weird dance videos or top-secret projects stay on your computer.

It even has a "Blur BG" mode for when you’re trying to make those portrait phone videos look decent on a landscape screen. 🌅
